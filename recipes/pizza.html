Pizza is an italian dish where cheese, sauce, and toppings of your choice are added onto a thin layer of bread.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Shreaded Chease

Instructions

  1. Roll out the dough into a cirlce around 1 cm thick
  2. Add sauce to the dough
  3. Add chease onto the sauce
  4. Put in oven
  5. Your done!
